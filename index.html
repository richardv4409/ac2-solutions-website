About AC2 Solutions

We operate differently

Founded in 2010 by Theo Christodoulou, AC2 Solutions is a premier financial advisory firm specializing in mergers and acquisitions, capital raising, empowerment transactions, and business turnarounds. With over 50 years of collective experience, our team delivers strategic, high-impact solutions across diverse sectors, including mining, retail, telecommunications, technology, and real estate.

The firm is led by Theo Christodoulou, a former Deutsche Bank veteran who spearheaded major empowerment deals and led the Mining & Resources team, AC2 combines deep industry expertise with an extensive network of relationships across industries. AC2 also enjoys strong relationships with commercial banks, development finance institutions, trade finance providers, asset managers, venture capital funds and high net worth family offices facilitating the raising of debt and equity capital for a broad range of projects including mining, real estate, venture capital, renewable and empowerment transactions.

Joined in 2024 by Richard Vaughan, former Eskom Group Treasurer and Edcon CFO, who has raised over R350 billion in debt and equity and led transformative restructurings, our team is uniquely positioned to deliver exceptional value.

Our Services

At AC2 Solutions, we deliver bespoke financial advisory services to empower businesses, investors, and shareholders to achieve their strategic and financial goals. Our expertise spans the following areas:

Mergers & Acquisitions

Sell-Side Mandates: We advise companies and shareholders seeking minority or majority investors or partial to full exits, maximizing value through strategic positioning and expert transaction execution.

Buy-Side Mandates: We assist trade buyers, private equity funds, and institutional investors in refining acquisition strategies, identifying high-value targets, and executing successful acquisitions.

Capital Raising

We secure debt and equity financing for established businesses seeking growth capital and select start-ups pursuing venture capital.


Our services include renegotiating lending facilities, obtaining bridging loans, and leveraging our extensive network of domestic commercial banks, development funds, and asset managers.

Empowerment

We structure and finance broad-based black economic empowerment (B-BBEE) transactions to deliver long-term strategic and financial value, connecting clients with empowered investors who align with their business vision.

Private Equity

We partner with private equity funds to optimize investment strategies, source high-potential opportunities, and execute complex transactions.


Our services include deal origination, structuring of leveraged buyouts and take-privates, and the raising of acquisition finance, drawing on our proven track record of delivering over R350 billion in debt and equity solutions.

Strategic Advisory

We provide independent, expert advice on capital structure, valuations, fairness opinions, and defense strategies. Our team supports boards and stakeholders with sector-specific insights across mining, retail, telecommunications, technology, and real estate, ensuring informed decision-making and optimized outcomes.

Business Turnarounds

We specialize in revitalizing businesses facing financial or operational challenges by developing and executing tailored turnaround plans, managing liquidity, optimising working capital, restructuring debt, streamlining operations, and engaging stakeholders to restore stability and drive sustainable growth.
